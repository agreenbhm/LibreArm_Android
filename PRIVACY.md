# Privacy Policy for LibreArm_Android

Effective date: December 17, 2025

LibreArm_Android is an open-source Android application that connects to a QardioArm blood pressure monitor over Bluetooth and optionally saves readings into Google Health Connect.  
This app is developed and maintained by independent contributors, and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Qardio, Inc.

---

## Information Collection and Use

LibreArm_Android does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal information outside of your device unless you choose to submit data to Google Health Connect.

- All blood pressure and heart rate readings remain **on your device**.
- Readings are optionally saved into the **Google Health Connect** if you grant permission.
- LibreArm_Android has no servers and no cloud storage.

---

## Health Data

- LibreArm requests permission to **write** the following data types to Google Health Connect:
    - Blood Pressure (systolic and diastolic)
    - Heart Rate
- LibreArm_Android does not read, share, or sell any Health Connect data.
- Health data access is managed entirely through Google's Health Connect APIs.

---

## Bluetooth Access

- LibreArm_Android requires Bluetooth access in order to connect to your QardioArm monitor.
- No other Bluetooth devices are scanned or connected.

---

## Data Sharing

- LibreArm_Android does **not** share your data with third parties.
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are included.

---

## Security

Because LibreArm_Android does not transmit or store data externally, your information is protected by the security of your device and Google Health Connect.
